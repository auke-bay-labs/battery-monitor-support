About

Battery Monitor estimates when your iPad will need charging by measuring your actual battery drain rate. It provides a real-time clock time showing when power will run out, along with a confidence indicator that improves over time.

Features

Real-Time Estimate

See the exact time your battery will run out, updated as conditions change.

Gets Smarter Over Time

Starts with a rough estimate, then measures actual drain for increasing accuracy.

Low Battery Alerts

Notifications at 30%, 20%, and 10% so you can plan ahead.

Widget & Live Activity

Home Screen widget and Lock Screen Live Activity for at-a-glance monitoring.

Completely Offline

No internet required. No data leaves your device. All data encrypted on-device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the percentage not match my status bar?

iPadOS reports battery level in approximate steps. The app interpolates between steps to get closer to the real value, but small differences are normal.

How long until the estimate is accurate?

The app shows an early estimate immediately on unplug. After 1-2 battery drops (typically 30-60 minutes), the estimate becomes reliable. After 4+ drops over an hour, it's highly accurate.

Does it drain my battery?

The app uses minimal resources. It listens for battery level changes rather than continuously polling, so its own power impact is negligible.
