Privacy Policy

Battery Monitor for iPad

Support Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

Overview

Battery Monitor is an iPad application that estimates remaining battery life. This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

Battery Monitor does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

The app operates entirely on-device. It does not:

  • Connect to the internet or any external servers
  • Transmit data off the device
  • Collect user identifiers, device identifiers, or analytics
  • Use advertising frameworks or tracking technologies
  • Access contacts, photos, location, camera, microphone, or any personal data

On-Device Data

The app reads the following device sensors to estimate battery life:

SensorPurposeStored?
Battery levelEstimate remaining runtimeOn-device only, encrypted
Battery charging stateDetect plug/unplug eventsOn-device only
Thermal stateDisplay in appNot stored
Screen brightnessDisplay in appNot stored
Low Power ModeDisplay in appNot stored
WiFi statusDisplay in appNot stored

All stored data (battery readings and drain rate history) is:

  • Encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM
  • Stored in the app's sandboxed container on the device
  • Never transmitted off the device
  • Automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled

Data Sharing

Battery Monitor shares data between the main app and its Home Screen widget using an App Group container. This is local, on-device communication — no data leaves the iPad.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from any user, including children.
